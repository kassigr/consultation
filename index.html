About Me

A Passion for Helping Professionals Thrive

With over 10 years of experience in high-pressure corporate environments, I understand the toll that chronic stress can take. My mission is to empower individuals and teams with practical, evidence-based strategies to manage stress, prevent burnout, and cultivate a healthier, more fulfilling work life.

My approach is rooted in mindfulness, cognitive-behavioral techniques, and positive psychology. I believe that stress management is not about eliminating stress entirely, but about building resilience and transforming your relationship with it.
